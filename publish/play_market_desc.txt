Solve 4clojure.com problems on your mobile phone.

It is time to test you Clojure knowledge and aptitude! 4Clojure is a collection
of Clojure koans (short programming challenges) of varying difficulty. Easy problems will help novices learn and understand Clojure better, while the hard will trouble even the experienced hackers.

Application synchronizes with 4clojure.com website and works in both online and offline mode. I thank http://4clojure.com team for developing and maintaining this great resource, and for allowing me to base the application upon their work.

4Clojure is written under Clojure-Android initiative. Source code for the application: https://github.com/alexander-yakushev/foreclojure-android
